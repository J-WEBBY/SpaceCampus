Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Introduction

At Space Campus, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and any details you provide when creating an account or filling out forms.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with the platform, including pages visited and features used.
  • Device Data: Technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the platform's features and services.
  • To personalize your user experience.
  • To communicate with you about updates, events, and support inquiries.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our offerings.

Data Sharing

Your data is only shared with trusted partners and service providers to facilitate the platform's functionality. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption, firewalls, and access controls. However, no system is completely secure, and we encourage users to take precautions with their accounts.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal information and request corrections.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and collect usage data. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. Users will be notified of significant updates.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us.
